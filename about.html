About me

Hi! My name is Alejandra Rivadeneyra. I am an industrial designer based in Lima - Perú. I studied at the Faculty of Arts at Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú.

I have been working for over a year in 3d printing and digital fabrication.

My objective with the fab academy is to learn more about electronics and programming so I can develop better in this field. Visit this website to see my work!

Little extra

I am also a dancer, and i do mostly folk dance. It has been hard for me to mantain this related to my career as a designer, as i don’t want to leave dance behind. I think it could be my first time linking these two activities by doing my Fab academy final project.
